# Financial Health Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project features an interactive monthly Power BI dashboard designed to provide leadership with a centralized view of the company’s financial health. It integrates P&L, Cash Flow, and Budgeting data to track KPIs across diverse regions and product lines.

## Problem Statement
The business lacked a unified view of financial performance. Key challenges included:
* **Data Silos:** P&L and Cash Flow data were managed separately.
* **Liquidity Blindspots:** Difficulty tracking net cash position in real-time.
* **Collection Delays:** No visibility into regional Receivables Aging.

## Logic & Methodology
1. **Data Engineering:** Standardized date tables to support **Time Intelligence** (YoY, YTD).
2. **DAX Modeling:** Developed measures for margin analysis and budget variance.
3. **Data Visualization:** Utilized trend lines, KPI cards, and decomposition trees for root-cause analysis.

##  Key Insights
* **Budget Variance:** Identified specific quarters where COGS exceeded budget by >15%, impacting Gross Margin.
* **Cash Flow:** Revealed a correlation between negative Net Cash months and slow regional collections.
* **Aging:** Pinpointed two specific regions responsible for 40% of overdue receivables.

##  Recommendations
* **Automated Collections:** Implement automated reminders for regions with aging >60 days.
* **COGS Audit:** Conduct a supplier review for product lines where revenue growth is decoupled from profit.
